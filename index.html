ARCHITECTURAL EVOLUTION

02 / 05
2G / 3G — INTELLIGENT NETWORK ERA
Circuit-Switched Voice & SS7 Signaling
Legacy IN architecture revolutionised telecom by decoupling service logic from switching hardware via SS7 / CAMEL. Prepaid telephony built on proprietary TSP/NSP hardware with DICOS real-time OS. The SDP-CCN pairing formed the backbone of real-time charging.

4G — LTE / EPC ERA
Packet-Switched Data & Diameter Charging
Explosion in data sessions forced migration to IP-based Diameter protocol. OCC introduced on Linux/IT stack to handle massive CCR/CCA flows from PGW. PCRF (now in CCPC) enabled dynamic QoS policy. Volume quota model replaced time-based reservations.

5G — STANDALONE SBA ERA
Cloud Native, HTTP/2, Service-Based Architecture
Diameter abandoned. Full HTTP/2 RESTful SBI across all functions. cCAF acts as CHF, CCPC as PCF. Containerised microservices on Kubernetes enable elastic scaling for massive IoT, URLLC, and network slicing. AI/ML-driven closed-loop KPI optimisation.

Dual-Mode Operation: Legacy + 5G Simultaneously
Real-world deployments maintain CCN/OCC alongside cCAF in converged configuration. A single monetization ecosystem simultaneously processes CAMEL IN traffic, 4G Diameter sessions, and 5G SBI charging. Migration of stateful SDP databases to cloud-native remains the primary engineering challenge.

KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

05 / 05
⬛
SYSTEM HEALTH
Transactions Per Second
TPS
Ultimate capacity metric. Measures CIP/SCAP/Nchf throughput. Critical during Hajj/Ramadan peak events where exponential traffic spikes demand pre-provisioned capacity.
CPU & Memory Load
CPU%
Sustained high CPU on CCN triggers capacity expansion. On cCAF Kubernetes deployments, CPU spikes auto-trigger horizontal pod autoscaling.
Signalling Latency
RTT
Round-trip for SDP reservation acknowledgement. Must remain in low milliseconds on legacy IN — MSC timeout causes dropped calls. Critical for URLLC in 5G (autonomous vehicles, industrial automation).
◈
NETWORK EFFECTIVENESS
Answer Seizure Ratio
ASR
% of successful call setups vs. total attempts. Drops in ASR flag CCN/SDP authorization failures — valid subscribers with sufficient funds being falsely barred due to DB corruption or protocol translation errors.
Network Effectiveness Ratio
NER
Isolates subscriber behaviour from network faults. Degrading NER with stable ASR → issue at OCC/CCPC policy layer. Sessions connecting but policies incorrectly dropping them.
VoNR Setup Success Rate
VoNR SSR
Nchf API signalling timeouts and VoNR setup failures are heavily scrutinised on cCAF/CCPC nodes. 5G voice relies on complex QoS flows managed by CCPC.
◉
USER EXPERIENCE
Time-to-Content
TTC
Delay before webpage/video renders. CCPC policy optimisation achieved 43% YouTube TTC reduction on WCDMA and 62% on LTE (dtac Thailand). Direct commercial impact on subscriber satisfaction.
Video Freezing Rate
VFR
Proxy for subscriber satisfaction. CCPC failure to provision dedicated bearer during congestion causes freezes. Proactive throttling of background downloaders protects real-time video streams.
Energy per Gigabyte
kWh/GB
Net Zero 2050 compliance KPI. Ericsson PME rApp uses AI/ML to detect performance degradation. CCPC manages UE sleep modes and slice activation to minimize RAN energy consumption and OPEX.
